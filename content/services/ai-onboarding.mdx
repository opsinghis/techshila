## What We Do

We embed AI-powered development workflows directly into your engineering teams. This isn't training — it's transformation. Your developers leave with production-ready agentic workflows that fundamentally change how they build software.

## Our Approach

### Assessment (Week 1)
We audit your current development lifecycle, identify bottlenecks, and map opportunities where AI agents can dramatically accelerate delivery.

### Installation (Week 2)
We configure and deploy customised agentic workflows — from AI-assisted code review to automated test generation — tailored to your tech stack and team structure.

### Enablement (Week 3-4)
Hands-on pairing with your engineers. We work alongside your team on real tickets, ensuring the workflows stick and the team is self-sufficient.

## What You Get

- **Custom agentic workflows** tuned to your codebase and processes
- **Team enablement** — your engineers own the workflows, not us
- **Measurable impact** — typical teams see 40-60% faster cycle times within 4 weeks
- **Documentation** — complete runbooks so your team can iterate independently

## Who It's For

Engineering teams (5-50 developers) who want to move beyond "vibe coding" to structured, repeatable AI-assisted delivery. Works with any tech stack.
