## The Challenge

A seed-stage fintech startup needed to go from concept to MVP in under 16 weeks to meet investor milestones. The founding team had domain expertise but limited engineering capacity.

## What We Did

### Architecture & Design (Weeks 1-2)
Designed a scalable platform architecture: Next.js frontend, Node.js API layer, PostgreSQL database, deployed on AWS with CI/CD from day one.

### Agile Delivery (Weeks 3-10)
Two-week sprints with continuous delivery. AI-augmented development meant one senior engineer had the output of a traditional team of three:
- AI-generated test suites for every feature
- Automated code review catching issues before human review
- Documentation generated alongside code

### Hardening & Launch (Weeks 11-12)
Security audit, performance optimisation, and production deployment. The platform launched on time with zero critical issues.

## Results

- **12-week delivery** — ahead of the 16-week target
- **94% test coverage** from day one
- **Successful fundraising** — the working platform helped close a Series A round
